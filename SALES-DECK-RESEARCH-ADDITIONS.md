## ðŸ’¡ KEY INSIGHTS FROM RESEARCH

1. **14 slides is optimal** (Kolide research) - We're close, don't bloat
2. **No agenda slides** - Jump straight into the problem
3. **Never give away the deck** - Keep control of narrative (use for training, not sending)
4. **Build emotion, not logic** - First meeting is about excitement, not closing
5. **Focus on 1-2 salient value props** - Don't try to sell everything
6. **End on a question** - Not "thank you", but "What challenge sounds most familiar?"
7. **Prospect is the hero** - We're Obi-Wan giving them the lightsaber, not the Jedi ourselves
