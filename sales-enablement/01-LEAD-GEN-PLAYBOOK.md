## üéØ Universal Lead Gen Tactics (Apply to All Tiers)

### **LinkedIn Strategies**

**Profile Optimization (Do This First):**
- Headline: "I help [industry] businesses [result]" (not "Sales Rep at Cold Lava")
- About section: 3-sentence value prop, link to case studies
- Featured section: Client success stories, demos, testimonials
- Activity: Share helpful content (not salesy) 3x/week

**Connection Request Strategy:**
- Send 10-20 personalized requests per day (LinkedIn limit)
- Mention something specific about their business
- NO pitch in the connection request (build rapport first)
- Follow up 2-3 days after they accept

**LinkedIn Content That Gets Responses:**
- "I just helped a [industry] business solve [problem] ‚Äî here's how..."
- "3 mistakes I see [industry] businesses making with [topic]..."
- Case study posts (client results, with their permission)
- Before/after comparisons

### **Cold Email Best Practices**

**Deliverability Basics:**
- Send from a real email (not "sales@", not "noreply@")
- Use your actual name (not "Cold Lava Sales Team")
- Keep it short (under 150 words)
- No links in first email (spam filters hate this)
- Personalize the first line (mention their company/industry/pain)

**Subject Line Formulas That Work:**
- Question: "How long does [pain] take you?"
- Curiosity: "Quick question about [Company Name]"
- Value: "[Result] in [timeframe]?"
- Specificity: "For [industry] businesses doing ¬£[X]M+"

**Email Cadence:**
- Day 1: Initial email
- Day 3: Follow-up #1 (add value, don't just "bump")
- Day 7: Follow-up #2 (case study or demo link)
- Day 14: Breakup email ("Should I take you off my list?")

### **Cold Calling Scripts**

**Opening (First 10 Seconds):**
```
"Hi [Name], this is [Your Name] from Cold Lava. I know I'm calling out of the blue ‚Äî do you have 30 seconds?"
```

### **Follow-Up System (Critical!)**

**The Rule:** 80% of sales happen after the 5th follow-up. Most reps give up after 1-2.

**Follow-Up Schedule:**
- **Day 0:** Initial outreach (email/LinkedIn/call)
- **Day 3:** Follow-up (add value: case study, tip, article)
- **Day 7:** Follow-up (different angle: demo link, competitor comparison)
- **Day 14:** Follow-up (social proof: "Just helped [similar company]...")
- **Day 21:** Breakup email ("Should I take you off my list?")

**If they ghost after a demo:**
- **Day 1:** Recap email (what we discussed, next steps)
- **Day 3:** Value add (case study of similar business)
- **Day 7:** Check-in ("Still interested or should I follow up later?")
- **Day 14:** Competitor angle ("Noticed [competitor] just did X...")
- **Day 21:** Breakup email

## ‚ùì FAQs

**Q: How many prospects should I contact per day?**
A: Aim for 20-30 NEW outreach attempts per day (mix of LinkedIn, email, calls). Plus follow-ups.

**Q: Which tier should I start with?**
A: Start with Tier 1 or 3 (Quick Wins or Websites) ‚Äî shorter sales cycles, easier to close early wins. Build confidence, then move up-market.

**Q: What if they don't reply?**
A: Follow up! Most people need 5-7 touches before they respond. Don't give up after 1 email.

**Q: Should I focus on one industry or spray and pray?**
A: Pick ONE industry per tier and become the expert. Easier to personalize, easier to build referrals.

**Q: How do I know if they're a good fit?**
A: Use the qualifying questions in each tier section. If they fail 2+ red flags, politely disqualify and move on.
